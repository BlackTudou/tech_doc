==================
video
==================

video 涉及模块：
 - 8-bit CIS(CMOS Image Sensor) DVP camera interface, 720p 25fps, 1080p 12fps
 - JPEG hardware encoder/decoder, support 1080p/720p
 - DMA2D controller
 - LCD interface (16-bit parallel RGB and I8080), 720p 30fps
 - SD/SDIO
 - full-speed USB OTG (FS)
 - 8 MB SiP PSRAM
